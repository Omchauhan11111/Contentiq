How it works

All config you set here is saved to MongoDB and sent to your n8n workflow as the payload when the workflow runs.

This means each user's content is completely isolated — the AI generates topics and posts based on your ICP, voice, and competitors only.

The schedule you set here tells n8n when to automatically run the workflow for your account (converted to UTC internally).

The webhook URL lets you manually trigger a run from the "Run Workflow" tab.

Run Workflow Manually
This sends your full profile config to n8n and starts the content generation process right now — without waiting for the scheduled time.
What happens when you trigger
1
Topic Generation — n8n fetches ESG news RSS feeds, scrapes competitor content, and uses Gemini AI to generate 30 scored topics based on your ICP and target region.
2
Review Window (24 hours) — Topics appear in your "Review Topics" tab. Green-light your top picks. After 24 hours, n8n auto-selects the top 5 by score.
3
Post Generation — For each approved topic, 3 LinkedIn post variations are written in your voice style, then QC-checked by AI.
4
Posts Ready — Posts appear in "Generated Posts" tab and you get a Slack/email notification. Copy the recommended version and publish!
